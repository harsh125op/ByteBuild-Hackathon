Manali, a stunning hill station in Himachal Pradesh, offers snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and adventure activities. From Solang Valley to Rohtang Pass, it’s perfect for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

Kashmir, the "Paradise on Earth," is known for its stunning valleys, snow-capped mountains, and serene lakes like Dal Lake and Gulmarg.
